Fun Facts!!

TIPS FOR THE OVERWORLD

  • Essential Tips

    • When your hunger bar is low, kill nearby animals like pigs, sheep, cows and chicken for food.

  • Survival Tips

    • Crafting a shield and equipping it in your off-hand with 'F' can help you use it quickly with just a right click, protecting you from things like projectiles and creeper explosions.

  • Efficiency Tips

    • A farm, even a simple wheat and animal farms, can help you easily gain resources without much effort.

  • Extras

    • Slimes only spawn in specific chunks, not based on biome. dirt,

TIPS FOR THE NETHER

  • Essential Tips

    • Bring a spare flint and steel to relight your portal incase it breaks.

  • Survival Tips

    • Wear any piece of gold armour to protect yourself from piglins.

  • Efficiency Tips

    • The Nether itself is an efficient way of travelling, with one (1) block in the Nether is equal to eight (8) blocks in the Overworld.

  • Extras

    • In a group of piglins, if one is hit, the others will also attack.

TIPS FOR THE END

  • Essential Tips

    • Be sure to destroy the end crystals on the obsidian towers to stop the ender dragon from healing themselves.

  • Survival Tips

    • Always have a waterbucket and enderpearls in your inventory when you're in the end. You will never know when you'll be shot in the sky.

  • Efficiency Tips

    • To deal more damage, use a bed's explosion as a weapon against the dragon. Like the Nether, beds in the End will also explode.

  • Extras

    • Shulkers can duplicate themselves if hit by a shulker's projectile.
