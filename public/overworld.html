THE OVERWORLD:

The Overworld is the main dimension where the player starts their world. This is the most familiar dimension in the game where most of their gameplay happens. This dimension features several events, which include the day-and-night cycle, dynamic weather, and a massive variety of biomes and creatures that come with it.

BIOMES:

Plains

The Plains is a flat area that could possibly spawn a few trees, and where animals are often found. They could also have a sunflower field variant, which is the only biome sunflowers spawn in.

– STRUCTURES • PASSIVE • MOBS –

Structures : Villages, Pillage Outposts.

Resources : Dirt Wood, Flowers.

Passive Mobs : Villagers, Cows, Sheep, Pigs, Chickens, Horses, Donkeys

Hostile Mobs : Zombies, Skeletons, Creepers, Pillagers
