THE NETHER:

The Nether is a dangerous and dark dimension that can be accessed through a Nether Portal, filled with hostile creatures, lava seas, and resources like no other. The Nether is an essential dimension that players have to go through to continue progressing. Though there are extra dangers that come with this new dimension, like beds exploding and water evaporating. The Nether only has one passive mob, which is the Strider that walks around lava and can be used by players with a saddle and a warped fungus on a stick

BIOMES:

Nether Wastes

Nether Wastes is the most common biome in the Nether. It is a massive biome made of netherrack, with its glowstone lights from the ceiling, and often has large oceans of lava surrounding it with shores of gravel and soul sand lining it. Nether quartz and Nether gold ore are seen on the surface, while Ancient Debris can be found underground.

– POSSIBLE STRUCTURES • HOSTILE MOBS –

Possible Structures : Nether Fortress, Ruined Nether Portal, Bastion Remnants

Hostile Mobs : Ghasts, Zombified Piglins, Piglins, Magma Cubes, Endermen

Soul Sand Valleys

Soul Sand Valleys are large areas covered in soul sand and soul soil, which can make the player’s speed slower. They often have basalt and soul fire spread across the biome. This is the only biome where you can obtain a dried ghast. This biome has nether fossils exposed around, with varied shapes and sizes.

– POSSIBLE STRUCTURES • HOSTILE MOBS –

Possible Structures : Bone Fossils, Nether Fortress, Ruined Portal

Hostile Mobs : Skeletons, Wither Skeletons, Ghasts, Endermen

Basalt Deltas

Basalt Deltas are areas that are often really dark and gray, a very unique terrain of basalt and blackstone. There are patches of lava puddles that make it more dangerous to explore if not proceeded with caution. The atmosphere in this biome is fog and dust that are spread inthe air. This is the only biome where nether bastions do not generate.

– POSSIBLE STRUCTURES • HOSTILE MOBS –

Possible Structures : Ruined Portal

Hostile Mobs : Magma Cubes, Ghasts, Zombified Piglins

Crimson Forests

Crimson Forests are large forests covered in colorful red trees and vines. The Crimson trees that cover the forests often have shroomlights that make the biome brighter. The floor consists of crimson nylium where red vegetation and fungi grow around the ground.

– POSSIBLE STRUCTURES • HOSTILE MOBS –

Possible Structures : Bastion Remnant

Hostile Mobs : Hoglins, Piglins, Piglin Brutes, Zombified Piglins

Warped Forests

Warped Forests are filled with large cyan fungi that also generate with shroomlights. The floor is made out of warped nylium that has warped vegetation and fungi that grow around it.

– POSSIBLE STRUCTURES • HOSTILE MOBS –

Possible Structures : Ruined Portal

Hostile Mobs : Endermen
